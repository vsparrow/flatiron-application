Why code?

Writing code will be as fundamental as writing on a peice of paper or typing. Those who cannot code will be left behind. With the rise of Internet-of-things (IoT), Virtual reality, 3-d printing, and robotics automation, the future will be dictated by those who can translate their will to and instruction set. In 1997 you had to call to get a pizza, go to Barnes & Nobles to get a book, and refer to the dewey decimal system to get information. Today all that can be done in the palm of your hand. I want to be part of the next 20 years, guiding technology rather than be made obselecent by it.
